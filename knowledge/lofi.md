# Genre: Lofi

## Characteristics
Lo-fi hip hop is defined by its deliberately imperfect, warm sound — vinyl crackle, muffled drums, jazzy chords. Energy is consistently low (0.25–0.55). Tempo is slow, typically 65–90 BPM.

## Mood associations
- **Chill**: The default lofi mood. Relaxed but mentally present.
- **Focused**: Study/work lofi — slightly higher energy than pure chill, mood is productive not sleepy.
- **Nostalgic**: Warm, bittersweet feeling common in lofi.

## Listener context
Lofi is almost always used as a background "focus soundtrack" — studying, coding, reading, late-night work. It is designed not to distract.

## Related genres
Ambient, jazz, chillhop. Lofi listeners often enjoy jazz instrumentals or ambient music.

## Recommendation tips
Acousticness is a strong signal — lofi tracks score 0.6–0.9. Energy similarity matters a lot here because the difference between 0.35 and 0.55 represents the difference between sleepy and productively focused.
