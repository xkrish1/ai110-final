# Genre: Rock

## Characteristics
Rock spans a wide range — from indie rock (energy 0.5) to metal (energy 0.95+). Guitar-driven, often with prominent drums. Tempo ranges widely: 90–180 BPM.

## Mood associations
- **Intense**: Classic hard rock / heavy metal territory. High energy, low acousticness.
- **Moody**: Alternative and grunge — mid energy, higher emotional complexity.
- **Relaxed**: Acoustic rock, classic rock ballads.

## Listener context
Intense rock fits workouts, driving, gaming. Moody rock suits late-night introspection. Acoustic rock fits casual listening.

## Related genres
Metal, punk, alternative, indie rock. Rock listeners wanting intensity often also enjoy EDM or synthwave.

## Recommendation tips
For intense rock profiles: energy similarity dominates. Genre match is critical — rock fans rarely want a pop song no matter how high-energy. Acousticness below 0.2 is a strong signal for hard rock preference.
