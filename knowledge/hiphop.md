# Genre: Hip-Hop

## Characteristics
Hip-hop is beat-driven with prominent bass and rhythmic vocals. Energy ranges widely (0.5–0.9). Danceability is typically high (0.75+). Tempo 80–110 BPM.

## Mood associations
- **Confident**: Assertive, self-assured — the most common hip-hop mood.
- **Intense**: Aggressive trap or drill — very high energy, fast hi-hats.
- **Moody**: Introspective rap — lower energy, more emotional depth.

## Listener context
Commutes, workouts, social settings, creative work. Hip-hop rewards active listening (lyrics) but also works as background.

## Related genres
R&B, trap, drill. Hip-hop and R&B listeners frequently overlap.

## Recommendation tips
Danceability is a strong differentiator. Confident/intense hip-hop fans rarely want acoustic or ambient songs regardless of energy overlap.
