# Genre: Ambient

## Characteristics
Ambient music prioritizes texture and atmosphere over melody or rhythm. Energy is very low (0.1–0.4). Tempo is slow or absent (50–75 BPM). Acousticness is high.

## Mood associations
- **Chill**: Peaceful, space-filling background sound.
- **Melancholy**: Darker ambient — drone-heavy, minor key.
- **Meditative**: Neutral, calming, used for sleep or mindfulness.

## Listener context
Sleep, meditation, deep focus, creative work. Ambient is the most "background" of all genres — it actively avoids demanding attention.

## Related genres
Lofi, classical, new age. Ambient listeners often enjoy lofi and acoustic genres.

## Recommendation tips
Valence and acousticness matter more than genre string-matching here. A user who wants ambient but their genre isn't in the catalog will be well-served by any song with energy < 0.35 and acousticness > 0.75.
